    I’m a data & graphics reporter on Chalkbeat’s data visuals team. Check out my recent bylines and my broader portfolio. I occasionally write blogs and freelance reviews about queer sci-fi and fantasy media.

    Previously, as a digital reporter at St. Louis Public Radio, I created graphics, built newsroom-wide tools, and covered data-driven stories across beats. Before that, I was an editorial assistant at St. Louis Magazine. I also produced freelance investigations, breaking news, web redesigns, and entertainment criticism, and spent a year in healthcare as a data analyst.

    In 2020, I co-founded the Trans Journalists Association with a collective of transgender and nonbinary media-makers; I also serve as a secretary for the St. Louis Pro chapter of the Society of Professional Journalists.
